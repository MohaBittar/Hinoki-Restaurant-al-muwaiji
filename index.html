About Us

The Story of Hinoki

Hinoki Restaurant brings the authentic flavors of Asia to Al Muwaiji. Our name, inspired by the Japanese cypress tree, reflects our commitment to natural elegance and timeless tradition in every dish we create.

From carefully selected ingredients to masterful culinary techniques, every plate tells a story of passion and dedication to the art of Asian cuisine.
